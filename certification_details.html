Certification Details

Certification Tracker

TCS encourages learning and empowers its people to learn and grow. We believe that continuous learning is the path for organisational and individual sustainability.

The Certification Tracker is one such step to tap your learning and help us align your aspirations with organisational needs. Completing relevant certifications will equip you to meet the expectations and challenges of the industry and help you have smooth journey from campus to corporate.

At TCSL, we believe that flexibility to work across technologies is imperative not only to meet our business requirements but also for the overall development of our employees. Exposure to diverse technologies during the initial phase will pave the way for a successful IT career ahead.

While the organization would consider your preference for technology streams basis the acquired certifications and locations submitted by you, the final decision will reside with TCSL and will depend on the organization's need to service a business requirement.

The certification list is divided into following two categories:

  • Star Certifications - TCS recognised and recommended Technical Certifications that are relevant for Initial Learning Program (ILP)
  • Ancillary Certifications - Other additional certifications recognised by TCS

Please read the following instructions carefully to update your certification/s:

  • Completing one or more Star Certifications will give you an additional advantage in terms of being considered for an advanced level of Initial Learning Program (ILP) focused on technologies in which you may have achieved certifications and getting the technology stream of your choice.
  • Note: This is applicable only to technical category of candidates who are offered and yet to receive their joining letters.
  • There exists a limited number of seats for each quarter (Apr-June, July-Sep, Oct-Dec, and Jan-Mar) for the number of star certified candidates who will be considered for advanced level of ILP. Candidates will be selected purely on first come first serve basis, that is, as per the date of submission of the certification details in NextStep.
  • If your submission of the certification details exceeds the quarterly limit, we may carry it forward to the subsequent quarter subject to business requirements of TCS.
  • You can select certifications only from the drop down list provided. The date of certification should be as mentioned on the certificate/relevant documents awarded to you.
  • In case of submission of more than one star certification details, you are required to indicate your preference by selecting the appropriate denomination from the drop down provided (For e.g.: If you have completed Java and .net certification, you would be required to indicate your first/second preference in terms of technology).
  • Request you to enter the details of acquired certifications only. You must possess valid proof of the certification and are required to upload the proof for each certification detail submitted. It is mandatory to carry the original certificate/relevant documents with you for verification at the time of joining.
  • Once the certification details are submitted it cannot be edited or deleted. Check the details entered before submitting.
  • You can continue to add to the list of acquired certifications till the time you join TCS.
  • The list of Star Certifications will undergo review and modification from time to time to include certifications relevant to the business of TCS.
  • Kindly note that certification expenses will not be reimbursed by TCS.
  • Acquiring the recommended certifications is purely at your sole discretion depending on your area of interest and technical inclination. TCS neither has a tie up with any professional body offering certification courses nor mandates Universities/Institutes to conduct/facilitate such courses.
  • Click on the below button to add/ update your certification details.
